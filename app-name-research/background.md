### Search: site:play.google.com "Jot" app

**Extremely crowded.** Multiple apps use the exact name "Jot":
- **Jot: Floating Notes & Notepad** - a note-taking app with floating window ([Jot: Floating Notes][1])
- **Boogie Board Jot** - official companion app for Boogie Board hardware ([Boogie Board Jot][2])
- **Jot** (encrypted journal) - encrypted, offline-first journal with AI ([Jot encrypted][3])
- **Jot - Notes and chats, with AI** - notes and conversations app ([Jot AI][4])
- **Jot App** - NFC-based profile sharing app ([Jot App][5])
- **Jot Journal** - AI-driven journaling app ([Jot Journal][6])
- Also: Jot ERP, JOT IT, Jott, Jotform

**Verdict: VERY CROWDED.** At least 6 apps with "Jot" as the primary name, several in the note-taking space directly.

---

### Search: "Jot" android app play store note taking

Confirmed the above findings. **Jot: Floating Notes & Notepad** appears to be the most prominent, with a floating window feature for quick note-taking. All notes stored locally. The app appears well-reviewed and actively maintained. This is a direct competitor in the same note-taking space.

### Search: site:play.google.com "Blurt" app

**Moderately crowded.** Found these apps:
- **Blurt: Blurting Study Method** - AI-powered study recall tool, 4.8 stars with 928 reviews, updated July 2025 ([Blurt Study][7])
- **BlurtKey** - wallet for Blurt blockchain accounts ([BlurtKey][8])
- Several business plan apps from a "blurt" developer (Business Ideas, Business Plan Quick Builder, etc.)
- Most other results were "Blur" apps (photo editors), not "Blurt"

Also found **blurt.app** exists as a web writing tool ([Blurt web app][9]), and there was a **Blurt audio social networking app** back in 2012 ([Blurt 2012][10]) that appears defunct.

**Verdict: MODERATELY CROWDED.** The name "Blurt" is taken by a well-rated study app, but fewer direct competitors than "Jot." Not in the note-taking space specifically, but the study app is adjacent. The blurt.app domain is also taken.

---

### Search: "Blurt" android app play store

Confirmed above. The **Blurt: Blurting Study Method** app is the main competitor - 4.8 rating, 928 reviews, actively maintained. BlurtKey is niche (blockchain). The Blurt blockchain social platform also has apps. Not as crowded as Jot but the name has existing associations.

- Also: Mama Murmur, MURMUR BEACH, aMurmur voice chat, Murmur Games

**Verdict: CROWDED, and one direct competitor.** The "Murmur - Voice Diary" app is extremely close to a voice-first note-taking app. This would be a problematic name collision.

**Verdict: VERY CROWDED.** QuillBot is a massive, well-known brand. Quillpad is an open-source note-taking app directly in the same space. The "Quill" concept is heavily associated with writing tools already.

### Search: "Quill" android app play store note writing

Confirmed above. Additional findings:
- **Quill** was also the name of a historical open-source handwriting note-taking app for Android tablets (now discontinued, available on GitHub)
- **Quillpad** is a fork of Quillnote, actively maintained, fully FOSS with markdown support, task lists, voice recordings, and Nextcloud sync
- **QuillBot** is a major AI writing assistant brand

The writing/note-taking space is saturated with "Quill" variants. This name would get lost.

**Very crowded in the note-taking space specifically.** Multiple direct competitors:

- **Jotter Mobile Working** - data capture/mobile working solution ([Jotter Mobile][32])
- **JotterPad - Writer, Screenplay** - professional text editor for writers, well-known app ([JotterPad][33])

**Verdict: VERY CROWDED in the exact note-taking space.** JotterPad is a well-established brand. Multiple other "Jotter" note apps exist or have existed. The word "jotter" literally means a small notebook, so it has been heavily used.

### Search: "Jotter" android app play store notes

Confirmed and expanded:
- **Jotter - AI Note Taking App** ([the-jotter.com][36]) - exists as a cross-platform AI note-taking app using Google Gemini for auto-categorization, available on iOS, Android, Web, WhatsApp, and Chrome extension
- The original **Jotter - Notes Writing App** is being discontinued
- **One Jotter** is free with unlimited notes

This name is extremely crowded in the exact use case we need. An AI-powered "Jotter" note-taking app already exists.
